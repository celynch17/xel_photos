Chris "XEL" Lynch

Started from some old shit now i'm here

A Maryland native, I moved to New Orleans, LA to attend Loyola University New Orleans. I picked up photography during college and have worked on perfecting my craft ever since.
I take and edit all of my own pictures. I am looking to turn my passion into a career. I take every picture to tell a story or capture one already in progress.
I find art where others often see nothing. Life is full of unrealized beauty and I use the camera to unveil its potential.
